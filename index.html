Predictive Tissue Stress Monitoring

Monitoring pressure before tissue damage begins.

PressureGuard is a low-cost predictive monitoring platform designed to analyze pressure exposure, duration, and tissue stress accumulation in real time. By combining continuous sensor input with temporal modeling, the system explores a more accessible approach to early pressure injury prevention.
